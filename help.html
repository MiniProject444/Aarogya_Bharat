Welcome to help tab of Aarogya Bharat website. We are here to help you assist you in navigating
through our platform seamlessly

For Patient
Simply login to your account by clicking on the patient button on the main webpage. If you don't have an
account then create an account by clicking on Sign Up and filling your basic information. After login, simply
search for the desired doctor or speciality, lick on the book and fill in the booking details.
That's it!! You have successfully booked an appoinment

For Doctor
Doctor's can view their appoinment by simply clicking on the Doctor button on the main webpage. Then simply login
to the account to display all the upcoming appoinment
